Brand Story


The Bakery was founded in 2022 by Tan Han Shen. He had a dream to open a bakery shop almost a decade. He works in many field but only this job will make his heart stay because he can feel he can deliver the heart of baking to many people. No matter rich or poor, happy or sad, customer will have the same heart to enjoy it.


The baker, Master Lee studies bakery in London and had experienced working at Van Stapple, Amsterdam, one of the famous bakeries in the world. Master Lee made his own recipes from scratch and you will be surprise when you taste the food he made.


While Mr. Yeong is responsible for providing the most comprehensive service to all customers. At the same moment, he also in charge of the quality of source of goods is in the good condition for the customers and also make sure the condition is the bakery is in hygiene. So every customer can enjoy their food with happiness.


Craftmanship' Spirit


From being familiar with every ingredient, to understand the role that various ingredients play in the baking process, and perfectly integrate these unique and individual ingredients to produce delectable cakes, breads, and pastries.


In the process of contact with the ingredients, Master Lee realized that only premium quality ingredients and putting in 100% of effort enable him to produce appetizing and delicious cakes, breads and pastries.


By using carefully selected premium quality ingredients to make various unique bakeries, we've let customers enjoy the smooth, tender and fragrant cakes, breads and pastries.


You will feel the happiness lies within with just one bite.


We are looking forward to serve the best baked foods in town.
